## Key Planning Highlights

* **4 Sprints:** The delivery plan was divided into **four focused iterations**: Governance & Setup, Variables & Dynamic Creation, Policy Logic & UX Action, and Deployment & Validation.
* **Velocity:** The project maintained a consistent delivery rate with an **average velocity of 4.75 story points per day** (19 total points delivered over 4 days).
* **Burndown Chart:** This visual tool tracks the **actual effort expended** against the **ideal rate of 4.75 points/day**, clearly validating the project's ability to meet the planned 4-day deadline.
* **User Stories:** All high-value tasks were formally defined, estimated with **Story Points**, and clearly mapped to team members for accountability across the sprints.
